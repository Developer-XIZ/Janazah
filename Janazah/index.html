ABOUT US

It can be very difficult for a small family and a small community of Muslims to keep up with the rites and the regular duties that are required when a loved one passes away. Still, these tasks must be carried out faithfully and here at Muslim Funeral Services of NY, we can help you make sure you and your community is not at sin by providing you with the leaders to lead your family in diligent prayer. Not only can we help you bury your deceased, but we also provide monthly grave-site visits as well.
We are a very knowledgeable and disciplined service here at Muslim Funeral Services of NY. We know the scriptures very well and we have been helping families bury their dead and honor their memories for a very long time. We are very familiar with the Muslim burial process as we have been in business for 12 years. If you have any questions or concerns about our services, it would be our honor to answer them for you. You can even call us 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
